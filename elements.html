Gecko facts

Geckos are a group of usually small, usually nocturnal lizards. They are found on every continent except Australia.

Many species of gecko have adhesive toe pads which enable them to climb walls and even windows. Some species, such as the Common house gecko Hemidactylus frenatus, have adapted to live in houses where they hunt small insects attracted by artificial light.
